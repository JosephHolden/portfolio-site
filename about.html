About

My Story

I specialise in Experience, Interface & Graphic design, but also have a wide ranging professional background, which I believe gives me a unique design perspective. I graduated from Lancaster University (UK) and began work in a law firm, as a paralegal working in civil litigation, and as the head of the client care department. I later relocated to Germany and managed a gastronomy company, which own two different gastro-bars, one of which I was asked to design the whole concept for.

With my combination of professional and creative experience I hope that I can bring another perspective to the evolution of interface design. I was really intrigued by what my design mentor once said; “where does design end?”. For me, the answer is that it doesn’t, it continuously evolves, and I’ so glad to be part of that evolution.
